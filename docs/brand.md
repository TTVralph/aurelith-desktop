# Aurelith Brand Guide (Early)

## Brand Position
Aurelith is an AI-first operating experience built around intent, clarity, and momentum.

## Product Personality
- Calm
- Precise
- Capable
- Futuristic

## Visual Direction
- Dark canvas with cool neon accents
- Dense-but-readable information cards
- High-contrast actions and status signals

## Voice & Tone
- Direct and useful
- Minimal fluff
- Action-oriented language

## Messaging Pillars
1. Command your digital life.
2. Turn intent into workflow.
3. Build momentum every day.
